Babito Bandito and ReD RuM announce the release of their latest single on Spotify

Sara N, courtesy

Babito Bandito and ReD RuM announce their collaboration for the single "No Retreat, No Surrender," inspired by the fusion of two alliances [PA13] THE PATROL 2.0 and [WSS] Why-So-Serious, released this month on Spotify. This collaboration is a single that will be part of an upcoming EP. Among the songs being eyed for an upcoming compilation are "You Know What?", the first track composed by Babito Bandito and ReD RuM.


The song: "No Retreat, No Surrender" is part of the Classic Rock genre. In an exclusive interview with Luxury Times, Babito Bandito revealed that the name for their single was chosen through public opinion polls in the alliance chat to which they belong.


"I asked the alliance for a name suggestion," Babito Bandito said. He mentioned that the creation began with a draft of ideas that he presented to ReD RuM. He added, "She wrote the lyrics, and I wrote the melody and put everything together."


ReD RuM added, "Babito asked me to help him write a song that captures the history of our alliance. 'No Retreat, No Surrender' is our anthem of unity, loyalty, and strength. We come from a different state, '94, outsiders at first, underestimated and overlooked. No one thought we were capable of reaching the level we have, but that's exactly what drove us," ReD RuM stated after discussing the challenges that have arisen since migrating to State 89 from State 94.


ReD RuM and Babito Bandito share the message they wish to convey through their music, particularly this song, which has been especially meaningful to both of them. They have captured deep feelings and thoughts, an unbreakable spirit, a declaration of struggle, and a display of resilience in each verse.


"This song was written as a reflection of our journey, our strength, unity, and loyalty. The lyrics aren't just about speed, steel, or the roar of engines, but about what it means to stand shoulder to shoulder with the people you trust, no matter the challenge. Each verse carries the spirit of resilience: being 'forged in fire,' riding through deserts, glaciers, and storms, is less about geography and more about life's battles, our struggles, our victories, and the bonds that cannot be broken. What made us even stronger was joining with another alliance. We listened to their story. It was similar to our own. That bond turned us from fighters into a force, gave us new strength, new clarity, and a new family and unity that inspired the chorus: 'We were born for this.' It's about knowing your purpose, knowing who you stand with, and never backing down from the fight for what matters most. At its core, this song is our anthem. It tells the world that we don't retreat, we don't give up, and we always will be Together, we are no longer the underdogs, but a family forged in trust, loyalty, and power," expressed ReD RuM.


Aside from their artistic side, we asked about their marital status. Between nervous laughter and a knowing tone, Babito Bandito simply said that he was a private person in these matters and would let his partner take the lead. "Let's just say we have a very special relationship. We're a team. We prefer to keep our personal lives private. Maybe we'll tell you that part of the story soon," said ReD RuM.


Among Babito Bandito's various projects are "Autumn" and the latest production, "End This Distances." The selection of singles continues to grow, and there are new collaborative projects in the works, all lovingly created for their listeners on their Spotify account, where you can find them under the stage name "BabOz," with a selection of more than 10 songs.
